How To Use?

Buttons`
[change text] - change your text that you should say,
[set time] - enter mili seconds, and your text words are slided in this time
[stop time] - stop timing,
[talk] - talk the word in the text and in the web should be shown the second word,
[stop] - stop recognising your voice,
[talk languages listBox] - select default languages to recognise your voice in selected language,
[change language] - enter the language that you should speak,
